What is MQRIC and Why Do You Need It?

MQRIC stands for the Malta Qualifications Recognition Information Centre. It's the official body that evaluates foreign qualifications and issues a statement on how they compare to the Malta Qualifications Framework (MQF).

For nurses applying to the Maltese Council for Nurses and Midwives, obtaining this statement is a mandatory first step. The Council's guidelines state that it is the applicant's responsibility "to produce a recognition statement on comparability of qualifications issued by the Malta Qualifications Recognition Information Centre (MQRIC)". Your qualification must be rated as at least MQF Level 5 to be considered valid.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Your MQRIC Statement

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal and Find the Application Link

Everything starts on the Malta Further & Higher Education Authority (MFHEA) website. Here is the link to begin:

https://mfhea.mt/academic-qualifications/

Once on the page, scroll down and click on the "Apply Here" button to start the process.

Step 2: Register and Enter Your Details

This is a two-part step:

  1. First, you will fill in your personal information to register on the portal. When asked, "Why is the statement required?", you should choose the option: "I want to apply for promotion/Job". Then click 'Next'.
  2. Next, you will provide your qualification details. Select your country of study (e.g., Poland), the name of your awarding institution (e.g., WyÅ¼sza SzkoÅ‚a Gospodarki), and the title of your qualification from the dropdown lists.

Important Note: If your university is in the dropdown list, your next steps are simple: click on add, check the confirm box, and move on to payment. You do not need to upload your degree or transcript. Document uploads are only required if your institution is not listed.

Step 3: Pay the Fee

The process requires a small fee. Based on my experience, the fee for a qualification already in the database was â‚¬15. You can pay this directly on the portal.

Step 4: Receive Your Statement Instantly

Because my qualification was recognized automatically, I received my official statement via email in about 10 minutes. It was a PDF document, ready to be saved for the next stage of my application.

What Your MQRIC Statement Looks Like

The final document is a formal statement of comparability. My own statement included:

  • A unique reference number (e.g., MQRIC 2025/33990).
  • My personal details and the name of my university.
  • The official title of my programme: "Bachelor of Science in Nursing".
  • The crucial evaluation result: "Comparable to MQF Level 6".

This Level 6 rating is the standard for a bachelor's degree and is exactly what you need for your professional registration.
