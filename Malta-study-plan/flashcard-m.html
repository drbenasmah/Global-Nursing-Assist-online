Day 1 & 2 – Flashcards

Why is teamwork important in healthcare?
Teamwork promotes safety, improves patient outcomes, and enables coordinated care. It ensures that patient needs are met through shared responsibility and clear communication.
What is the Maltese Code of Ethics for nurses?
It outlines the ethical responsibilities of nurses in Malta — including patient advocacy, respect for dignity, accountability, professional conduct, and collaboration with colleagues.
Name the 5 Moments of Hand Hygiene.
1. Before touching a patient
2. Before a clean/aseptic procedure
3. After body fluid exposure risk
4. After touching a patient
5. After touching patient surroundings
