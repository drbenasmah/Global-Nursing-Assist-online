Day 7: Week 1 Review

Review all flashcards from the week. Pick 10 topics and explain each one out loud using the SAFE/SBAR framework.

Day 8-9: Scenario Practice

Practice verbalizing answers to recall questions (Fall, Confused Patient, Post-Op checks, etc.) using the SAFE/SBAR framework.

Day 10: Ethics & Communication

Practice scenarios involving informed consent, ethical dilemmas, and communicating with anxious family members.

Day 11: Mock Interview #1 (Self)

Record yourself answering 10-15 questions. Critique your performance on clarity, structure, and timing.

Day 12: Refine Weak Spots

Based on your mock interview, re-study the topics you were hesitant on. Focus on fluency.

Day 13: Dress Rehearsal & "Bookends"

Prepare your intro & questions. Do a final mock interview in your interview clothes and setting.

Day 14: Final Review & REST

Do a final, light read-through of your notes. Then stop. Relax, get good sleep, and trust your preparation.
